Privacy Policy

App name: MessengerLogger

What data we collect

MessengerLogger collects messages sent by users to our Facebook Page through Messenger. This may include message text, sender Page-scoped ID, timestamps, and Messenger event metadata.

How we use the data

We use this data only to receive, display, and log Messenger messages for support, testing, and customer communication purposes.

Data sharing

We do not sell user data. We do not share Messenger message data with third parties except when required by law.

Data storage

Messages may be stored in application logs or a database for operational purposes.

Data deletion

Users can request deletion of their Messenger data by contacting us at: zayarkasparov@gmail.com
